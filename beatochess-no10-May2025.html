Waenink +0444.54a6a8 (1985). Cook, correction (Polasek)

Date: May 22, 2025

File: Waenink1985a.pgn

White cannot win at the end, but things are tricky.

The winning position has the white bishops on d8 and e7 and the black bishop on c7, and importantly, Black to move! The position can be reached in Waeninks study, but only with White to move, which draws as follows: Bf6 Be5! Bde7 Bf4 Bf8 Bc7! B6e7 Kb8! etc.

Jaroslav Polasek came up with a nice correction, in which the critical position is reached with Black to move.
